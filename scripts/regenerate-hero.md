# Hero Image Regeneration Guide (Tarjeta 2 — Espacio)

This document outlines the requirements and process for replacing the outdated, raw concrete floor hero image (`sillon.jpeg`) with a brand-aligned, high-luxury aesthetic alternative (`sillon-v2.jpeg`).

## Objective
To replace `public/sillon.jpeg` (which features an unrefined raw concrete floor that breaks our sleek dark-mode brand tone) with a premium-quality rendering of an armchair emerging from darkness, suspended in a pure black void.

## Target Output Specifications
- **Format**: JPG or PNG (saved to `public/sillon-v2.jpeg`)
- **Aspect Ratio**: Square 1:1
- **Dimensions**: 1200 × 1200 pixels (or higher, e.g., 2048 × 2048)

## Recommended AI Tools
- **Ideogram** (Recommended for superior prompt compliance and high contrast control)
- **Leonardo.ai** (Alternative, using cinematic/fantasy models for rich fabric details)

## Exact Prompt to Use
```text
Cinematic dark studio. A single elegant armchair in warm amber velvet fabric 
emerging from pure darkness on the right side. Dramatic side lighting casting 
long shadows, perfect interior photography quality. NO concrete floor visible — 
deep black void below. Left side pure black. Square format 1:1. Editorial luxury style.
```
